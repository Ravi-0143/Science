# Atlas Glossary

**Version:** 1.0

---

* **Atlas Experience:** An interactive, simulation-driven learning unit built around a single scientific theme.
* **Cognitive Resonance:** Alignment between an abstract concept and an intuitive spatial/physical metaphor.
* **Scene:** An uninterrupted spatial and narrative segment focused on a single sub-concept.
* **Hero Canvas:** The primary spatial stage where simulation, manipulation, and observation occur.
* **Interaction Density:** The degree of active physical or logical control available to the user per minute of engagement.
* **Mental Model:** The internal cognitive representation a user constructs to predict how a system functions.
