## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

* **The Animated Textbook:** Presenting a static block of text alongside an un-interactive loop of an animation.
* **The Button Masher:** Interactions that merely step through a linear slide deck ("Click Next to continue").
* **The Black Box:** A simulation that produces an outcome without revealing *why* or *how* the variables interacted to produce it.
* **The Point System:** Rewarding progress with arbitrary badges or scores instead of relying on the intrinsic joy of discovery.
* **The Gimmick:** Using impressive 3D visuals or visual effects that distract from—rather than illuminate—the core scientific phenomenon.
