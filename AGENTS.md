## ⚡ Core Rules of Engagement for AI Agents

1. **Constitutional Primacy**: When any requirement is ambiguous or conflicting, [`00_CONSTITUTION.md`](00_CONSTITUTION.md) always supersedes.
2. **Strict Boundary Adherence**: Stay strictly within your assigned role responsibility defined in `roles/`. Never modify files outside your role scope.
3. **Experience Isolation**: Science experiences must be self-contained and adhere to [`05_EXPERIENCE_CONTRACT.md`](05_EXPERIENCE_CONTRACT.md). Never couple the platform core to specific content.
4. **Quality & Anti-Patterns**: Every line of code must pass the criteria in [`06_QUALITY_BAR.md`](06_QUALITY_BAR.md) and avoid all anti-patterns listed in [`08_ANTI_PATTERNS.md`](08_ANTI_PATTERNS.md).
