CBSE Optics Formula Summary

• Lens Formula: 1/f = 1/v - 1/u  |  Magnification: m = v / u = h_i / h_o
• Mirror Formula: 1/f = 1/v + 1/u  |  Magnification: m = -v / u = h_i / h_o
• Sign Convention: Object distance $u$ is always negative. Focal length $f$ is positive for Convex lens/mirror and negative for Concave lens/mirror.
